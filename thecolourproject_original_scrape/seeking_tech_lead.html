Seeking: Technical Leads

We are looking for Technical Leads to join our team!


We are a small team comprised of individuals with a desire to make a difference in the mental health space, and TCP is very much a shared passion project. We are looking for several experienced Technical Leads to join us in a skills-based volunteering capacity and who can help form a core part of the team.


Estimate of hours will vary and it may be safer for us to provide a general overview of the type of support needed as hours required will depend on experience (note: Technical Leads do not have to have ALL of the below - we open to those able to support front-end or back-end, or both):


  • Comfort with ReactJS, Ruby, Rails, and JavaScript in general (our current stack is based around Ruby on Rails and React Native)
  • Experience building and maintaining smartphone apps (both Android and iOS platforms)
  • Some experience in website operations and infrastructure
  • Beyond technical skills, we are looking for someone who shares a similar drive toward creating a more supportive and colourful world, free of stigmatization, for those living with mental illness

If you or anyone you know may have the experience, availability and desire to join, please email Amanda and Ian at hello@thecolourproject.ca. We would love to connect with you!
